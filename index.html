Research Interest

I am exploring the field theoretical aspect of quantum fluctuations generated during inflation in the early Universe.

Keywords: Cosmic Inflation, Cosmological Perturbation, Quantum Field Theory in Curved Spacetime, and Cosmological Bootstrap.
